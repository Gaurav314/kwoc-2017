— About Event
BY THE COMMUNITY, FOR THE COMMUNITY

The true value of Open Source is People. People, like the ones who care to solve problems not just for their own good but for the community. People, like the ones who share the passion to learn indefinitely. Let's get together and listen to the ideas of the very same people, about Open Source. And also, Software!

— Event Speakers

The event speakers are Kharagpur Winter of Code 2017 mentors who dedicate a great deal of their time to help beginners into open source and share a common passion for the community. Our Keynote address is by a Guest Speaker who can often be seen with a wise notebook of "values" and experiences in following Open Source for a long time.
